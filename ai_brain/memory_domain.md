# Domain Memory — AI Marketing, Psychology, CRO

## 1. Core Domains

### AI Marketing

- AI tools for:

  - content generation

  - personalization

  - media buying

  - analytics & forecasting

- AI marketing stacks for 2025–2027.

- Differences between:

  - AI-assisted vs AI-first marketing.

- Trend focus:

  - Predictive AI

  - Agentic AI

  - Real-time optimization

### Consumer & Behavioral Psychology

- Decision psychology:

  - friction, hesitation, cognitive biases.

- Emotional triggers:

  - fear, desire, trust, loss aversion, curiosity.

- Behavioral funnels:

  - awareness → consideration → decision → post-purchase.

- Concepts:

  - emotional resonance, attention patterns, mental availability.

### CRO & Copywriting

- Conversion-focused copywriting:

  - clarity, contrast, proof, urgency, relevance.

- A/B testing:

  - hypotheses, variants, metrics, interpretation.

- Landing page structure:

  - hero, social proof, problem, solution, benefits, objections, CTA.

- CRO metrics:

  - CR, CTR, bounce, scroll depth, micro-conversions.

### AI + Psychology Integration

- Emotion AI:

  - detecting sentiment, emotional tone, and intent.

- Personality models:

  - how traits influence messaging, UX, and offers.

- AI behavioral segmentation:

  - clustering users by patterns, not just demographics.

## 2. How the AI Should Think with This Domain Memory

- Always combine:

  - AI capabilities  + human psychology  + business impact.

- When asked for "advice", respond like a **consultant**, not a blogger.

- When writing content:

  - Make it both **SEO-ready** and **conversion-aware**.

- When planning strategy:

  - Start from **behavior & psychology**, then tools, not the other way around.
