Why Decision Diagnosis Matters

Most conversion problems aren't design problems. They're decision problems. Users hesitate because something breaks psychologically at the moment of decision — not because your button is the wrong color.

One wrong decision can cost more than this diagnosis.

We diagnose the psychological barrier preventing decisions. You get clarity on what to fix first — before spending on redesign, A/B tests, or ad campaigns.
